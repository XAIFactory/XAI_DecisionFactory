# XAI_DecsionFactory
Turn ML outputs into actionable insights with semi-global explainability and dual-agent HITL intelligence.
XAI_DecisionFactory is an Explainable AI framework for regression and classification models that combines:

Statistical Layer – analyzes model outputs to identify semi-global patterns, robust segments, and feature contributions.
Agentic Layer with Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) – consists of two specialized agents:
Context Agent: interprets model context, purpose, and objectives, ensuring the insights are aligned with business or research goals.
Analytical Agent: performs analytical reasoning on statistical outputs, producing interpretable profiles, actionable insights, and recommendations. Human feedback ensures hallucination control and refinement of results.

This two-step, dual-agent architecture ensures your models are not only technically transparent, but also decision-ready, bridging the gap between data science outputs and real-world actions.

Semi-global explainability: focuses on meaningful segments rather than single predictions or entire models
Actionable insights: converts model patterns into concrete recommendations
Dual-agent HITL output: produces human-validated, context-aware profiles and guidance informed by model purpose and objectives
